Red Fort (Delhi)

Constructed by Shah Jahan when he shifted his capital to Delhi, this massive red sandstone fortress served as the main residence of Mughal Emperors for nearly 200 years. Today, it is a symbol of Indian sovereignty, where the Prime Minister hoists the national flag every Independence Day.
