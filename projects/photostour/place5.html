Golden Temple (Amritsar)

Also known as Sri Harmandir Sahib, it is the holiest shrine in Sikhism. Its upper floors are covered in 750kg of pure gold. The temple is famous for its spirit of equality and its Langar (community kitchen), which serves free meals to over 100,000 people daily, regardless of their religion or background.
