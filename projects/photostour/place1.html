Charminar(Hyderabad)

Built in 1591 by Sultan Muhammad Quli Qutb Shah, this "Mosque of the Four Minarets" is the global icon of Hyderabad. Legend says it was built to commemorate the end of a deadly plague. It is famous for its Indo-Islamic architecture and the bustling Laad Bazaar that surrounds it.
