Golconda Fort (Hyderabad)

Originally a mud fort built by the Kakatiyas, it became a massive granite citadel under the Qutb Shahi kings. It is world-renowned for its acoustic marvels (a clap at the entrance can be heard at the top of the hill) and was once the center of the world's diamond trade, producing the Koh-i-Noor diamond.
