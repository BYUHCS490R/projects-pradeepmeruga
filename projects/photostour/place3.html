Taj Mahal (Agra)

A UNESCO World Heritage site and one of the New Seven Wonders of the World. This ivory-white marble mausoleum was commissioned in 1632 by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife, Mumtaz Mahal. It is considered the finest example of Mughal architecture, representing eternal love.
