Important Story Arcs

One Piece has many exciting story arcs. Each arc introduces new characters, new islands, and new challenges for the crew.

East Blue Arc

This is where Luffy begins his journey and starts gathering his first crew members.

Alabasta Arc

The crew travels to Alabasta to help Princess Vivi save her kingdom from danger.

Marineford Arc

This is one of the most emotional arcs in the series, with major battles and important events.

Wano Arc

In Wano, the Straw Hats fight powerful enemies and help free the land from oppression.
