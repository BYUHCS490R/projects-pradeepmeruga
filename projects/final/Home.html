Welcome to One Piece

One Piece is one of the most popular anime and manga series in the world. It follows Monkey D. Luffy and his crew as they travel across the sea in search of the legendary treasure called One Piece.

The story is full of action, adventure, friendship, and dreams. This website gives fans a simple guide to the characters, important story arcs, Devil Fruits, and the Straw Hat crew.

  • Important story arcs in the series
  • Different types of Devil Fruits
  • A table showing Straw Hat crew information
  • A fan form to share your favorite parts of the series
