What Are Devil Fruits?

Devil Fruits are special fruits in One Piece that give people unique powers. However, the person who eats one loses the ability to swim.

Paramecia

Paramecia fruits give unusual abilities that can affect the body, objects, or the environment.

Zoan

Zoan fruits allow the user to transform into an animal or an animal-human form.

Logia

Logia fruits give control over natural elements such as fire, smoke, or light.

Examples

Luffy fruit gives him rubber-like abilities. Other fruits in the series can create fire, ice, or even allow transformation into animals.
