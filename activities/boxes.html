Engagement

  1. Block tags start on a new line and usually take up the whole width of the page. They are used for main sections like paragraphs, headings, or divs. Inline tags stay on the same line with other text and only take the space they need, like links, bold text, or spans.
  2. These are the spaces around an element. Padding is the space between the content and the border. The border is the line around the content. Margin is the space outside the border that separates the element from other elements on the page.
  3. Some common CSS units are px, %, em, rem, vw, and vh. Pixels are fixed sizes, while %, em, and rem change based on the screen or text size.
  4. we use a figure tag when we want to place an image, chart, or diagram together with a caption so they stay grouped and organized.
